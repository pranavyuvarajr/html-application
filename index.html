Bleach Anime

Bleach is a Japanese anime and manga series written and illustrated by Tite Kubo. The story follows Ichigo Kurosaki, a teenager who accidentally acquires the powers of a Soul Reaper—a death personification similar to the Grim Reaper—from another Soul Reaper, Rukia Kuchiki. His new powers task him with defending humans from evil spirits and guiding departed souls to the afterlife.
