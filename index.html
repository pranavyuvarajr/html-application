Welcome to the World of Soul Reapers

Dive into the epic world of Bleach, follow Ichigo Kurosaki as he battles hollows and protects the balance between the living world and the Soul Society.

Main Characters

Ichigo Kurosaki

The protagonist who accidentally gains Soul Reaper powers to protect humans from evil spirits.

Rukia Kuchiki

A Soul Reaper who gives Ichigo his powers and guides him in his journey.

Byakuya Kuchiki

The stoic captain of the 6th Division in the Soul Society, master of Senbonzakura.

Orihime Inoue

Ichigo's classmate with unique healing powers and strong loyalty to friends.
